Frequently Asked Questions

What is GenAI safety and security, and why is it important?
GenAI safety and security refers to the practices and technologies used to protect generative AI systems from threats and vulnerabilities with a defensive mindset. We prioritize user safety, including identity protection, data privacy, and psychological well-being. As GenAI becomes more prevalent, ensuring its responsible and secure use is crucial for individuals and society as a whole.
How often is your newsletter published?
Our newsletter is published weekly with technical reviews, providing timely updates on the latest developments in GenAI security. Each issue contains expert analysis, practical tips, and industry insights, drawing from our extensive experience in AI, cybersecurity, and emergent technologies.
What's the background of the author behind this newsletter?
The author has a diverse background spanning social media management, graphic design, academic research in information systems, and frontier AI research with Healthcare, Education and Climate Change innovation. She was internationally recognized as an award winner from US, Canada, Switzerland and global communities. With experience in both industry and academia, including work on privacy, security, and Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), the author brings a unique perspective to GenAI safety and security issues.
How did this newsletter get started?
After experiencing the potential misuse of data in social media and recognizing the rapid advancement of GenAI, the author felt compelled to address the critical issues of AI safety and security. The newsletter aims to share insights and promote responsible AI development and use.
What is the goal of this newsletter?
The primary goal of this newsletter is to provide in-depth analysis of emerging trends in GenAI safety and security. We aim to:
  1. Bridge the gap between complex AI developments and practical, actionable insights.
  2. Raise awareness about potential misuse of AI, including scams and manipulation.
  3. Promote responsible AI development and use.
  4. Educate readers on protecting themselves in an AI-driven world.
  5. Foster a community focused on ethical AI practices and social well-being.
Why focus on AI security and privacy?
Recent personal experiences with online scams and identity theft, combined with professional insights into AI capabilities, have highlighted the urgent need for improved AI security and privacy measures. As GenAI becomes more sophisticated, the potential for misuse grows. This newsletter aims to stay ahead of these challenges and help readers protect themselves and their data.
What can readers expect from this newsletter?
Subscribers can look forward to:
  1. Weekly issues with the latest insights on GenAI security and privacy.
  2. In-depth analyses of emerging AI threats and vulnerabilities.
  3. Practical tips for protecting yourself in an AI-driven world.
  4. Discussions on the ethical implications of AI development and use.
  5. Updates on relevant regulations and policies (e.g., EU AI Act, UN AI resolutions).
  6. Personal anecdotes and lessons learned from the author's experiences in the field.
